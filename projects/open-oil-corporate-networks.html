Description

OpenOil was founded in the belief that creative, practical and socially progressive policy making in the oil and gas industries is both vital to our collective future, and possible within current constraints.

Approach (How?) : The Open Oil Corporate Networks system uses interoperable data formats, clear editorial standards, and public domain data to create network graphs of companies and entities in the oil, gas and mining industries.
