About the Influence Mapping Toolbox

The influence mappers toolbox brings together resources to help you

  • Identify data for your project
  • Organise your data
  • Make sense of your data
  • Present your data and findings

The Toolbox has been developped by iilab - information innovation lab as part of the Influence Mapping project.
