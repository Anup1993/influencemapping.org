Description

Cargografias is an experimental visualization tool that reveals the careers of the politicians inside the state.

Approach (How?) : By mapping the duration of electoral mandates of all Argentinian political representatives, by provinding statistics about their number of political mandates, Cargografias provides a more transparent picture of Argentiian politics before the next presidential elections.
