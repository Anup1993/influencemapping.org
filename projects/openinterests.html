Description

OpenInterests catalogues actors involved in lobbying, expert groups, public expenditure and procurement so you can explore the interactions between them.

Approach (How?) : Listing data from a number of sources, OpenInterests is a search engine for information about and links between the political and commercial actors around the European Union
