Description

Connected China tracks and visualizes the people, institutions and relationships that form China's elite power structure. Launched in February 2013, the app provides deep insight into China’s new generation of leaders and features the best of Reuters’ coverage in data, text, photos and video. It is optimized for iPad 2+ and modern browsers - Chrome and Safari recommended for best user experience.

Approach (How?) : Connected China shows how China's elite is structured through relational data and identifies who is pulling the strings in the PRC with infovisualization and narratives.
