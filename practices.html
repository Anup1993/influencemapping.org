Practices

List of practices

Planning

When you get started on a project it's usually an inspiration or a question that guides you, but to achieve your goals, a little bit of planning goes a long way.

  • Getting Started: Get started on an influence mapping project

  • Project Design: Define your problem and structure your project

  • Ethics: Do the right thing

  • Collaboration: Find the right collaborators

  • Planning: Devise a way to achieve your goals

Collecting

There's a lot of data out there asking the right questions and knowing when you need to organise your own data collection takes experience.

  • Getting Started: Asking the right questions to get the right data

  • Finding Data Sources: Find good data sources

  • Collecting Data: Organise the collection of data

  • Data Quality: How to check your data quality?

Organising

Where and how you're going to organise or structure your data is key to determine how your project is experienced by your audience but it will also have a lot of impact on the type of technology you'll need.

  • Getting Started: Getting to know your data

  • Data Modeling: How to choose a model to organise your data

  • Data Integration: Joining data from different sources

  • Hosting & Security: Where is your data going to be stored and who will be able to access it

Analysing

Investigating, analysing and finding more about your problem through your data is the key process through which your story or your project will take its definitive shape.

  • Getting Started: Are the answers to your questions or the links you want to put in the spotlight in your data?

  • Verifying Data: How to verify your data?

  • Cleaning Data: How to clean up your data?

  • Analyse Data: How to analyse your data?

Publishing

Putting your story out there is when it can be able to create impact. But with data stories, allowing others to build on your result can be the difference between satisfying accolades by your peers and actual real world impact.

  • Getting Started: What are the ways in which your result and your data can have the impact you want?

  • Data Narratives: Telling a story with your data

  • Describing the Data: Explain where your data is coming from and how its structure

  • Data Reuse: Make it easier for others to reuse and build on your data

  • Impact: Know when you're having the impact you were hoping for
