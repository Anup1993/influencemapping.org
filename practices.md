### Organise
_Investigating, analysing and finding more about your problem goes hand in hand with how you organise and structure your data. It determines how your project is experienced by your audience but it will also have a lot of impact on the type of technology you&#39;ll need._
<br>
<br>
  * [Get Started](practices/subpractices/get-started.html): _Getting to know your data. Are the answers to your questions or the links you want to put in the spotlight in your data?_ 

  * [Model Data](practices/subpractices/model-data.html): _How to choose a model to organise your data_ 

  * [Integrate Data](practices/subpractices/integrate-data.html): _Transforming and joining data from different sources_ 

  * [Analyse Data](practices/subpractices/analyse-data.html): _How to analyse your data?_ 

  * [Host &amp; Secure Data](practices/subpractices/host-secure-data.html): _Where is your data going to be stored and who will be able to access it_

### Publish
_Putting your story out there is when it can be able to create impact. But with data stories, allowing others to build on your result can be the difference between satisfying accolades by your peers and actual real world impact._
<br>
<br>
  * [Get Started](practices/subpractices/get-started.html): _What are the ways in which your result and your data can have the impact you want?_ 

  * [Present Data](practices/subpractices/present-data.html): _Make your data tell its story and engage your audience_ 

  * [Describe Data](practices/subpractices/describe-data.html): _Explain where your data is coming from and how its structure_ 

  * [Facilitate Data Reuse](practices/subpractices/facilitate-data-reuse.html): _Make it easier for others to reuse and build on your data_ 

  * [Measure Impact](practices/subpractices/measure-impact.html): _Know when you&#39;re having the impact you were hoping for_
