Description

A platform that enables you to view edits and history of controversial topics on Wikipedia

Approach (How?) : This research project -- currently still a demo version -- lets you explore and visualizes Wikipedia controversial articles by browsing through edits and changes stemming from different opinions and ideological agendas. With this research project, not only one can better understand the arguments claimed by the different parties but also can grasp how democratic debates get influenced by particular interests.
