Siyazana

We know each other

Keywords : database, politics, corporations, south africa, grano,

Thematic Focus : Exposure

Geographic Focus : South Africa

Experience : Lists

Description

Siyazana provides users with a tool to investigate the connections that exist between the private and public spheres in South Africa. Specifically, it provides a unique insight into the financial connectivities that can, and often do, determine how, when, and why money is expended by government.

Approach (How?) : Draws connections between corporate entities and politicians in South Africa by providing lists of connections.

Language : English

Partners : Institute for Security Studies, African Network of Centers for Investigative Reporting, International Center for Journalists.
