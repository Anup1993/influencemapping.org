# Projects

List of projects


  * [Cargografías](projects/cargografas.html)
    <p> Cargografias allows you to explore the timelines of the electoral mandates of the Argentinian representatives.  </p>

  * [La Fabrique de La loi](projects/la-fabrique-de-la-loi.html)
    <p> La Fabrique de La loi tracks the evolution of the lawmaking and votation process in the French parliament

 </p>

  * [Trade Advisory Commitee and Industry relationships](projects/trade-advisory-commitee-and-industry-relationships.html)
    <p> Discover the profiles of the Obama administration&#39;s corporate-heavy network of official trade advisers </p>

  * [Map the Banks](projects/map-the-banks.html)
    <p> Map the Banks shows companies licensed to carry out financial activities all over the world  </p>

  * [Poderopedia](projects/poderopedia.html)
    <p> With Poderopedia, you can explore stories and visualize the acquaintances of the elite  </p>

  * [Resource Contracts](projects/resource-contracts.html)
    <p> Resource contracts is a database of mining, oil and pretroleum contracts from all around the world </p>

  * [OpenDuka](projects/openduka.html)
    <p> Open Duka is a free repository of data regarding Kenyan ownership information regarding entities, institutions </p>

  * [Presidential campaigns and staff connections ](projects/presidential-campaigns-and-staff-connections.html)
    <p> This NYT visualizations allows you to learn more about the work trajectory of the presidential candidates&#39; current staff </p>

  * [Connected China](projects/connected-china.html)
    <p> Connected China maps out relationships of the Chinese elite power </p>

  * [Quien Manda](projects/quien-manda.html)
    <p> Quien Manda documents and maps relationships of the Spanish elite </p>

  * [Muckety](projects/muckety.html)
    <p> Muckety reveals connections between wealthy and influential individuals and big corporations in the world </p>

  * [Who do they Serve](projects/who-do-they-serve.html)
    <p> Who do They Serve shows relationships within powerful elite systems </p>

  * [Swiss Leaks](projects/swiss-leaks.html)
    <p> The International Consortium of Investigative Journalists (ICIJ) analysis of leaked files of one of the world’s most private banks : HSBC </p>

  * [Lobby Facts](projects/lobby-facts.html)
    <p> Lobby Facts is a repository of information regarding the lobbies present in the Brussels parliament  </p>

  * [Influence Explorer](projects/influence-explorer.html)
    <p> Influence Explorer lets you browse those who have contributed to US campaign finances </p>

  * [The counted](projects/the-counted.html)
    <p> The Guardian created the first online database of police killings in the USA </p>

  * [The Next to Die](projects/the-next-to-die.html)
    <p> The Next to Die sheds light on upcoming executions in US states </p>

  * [Contropedia](projects/contropedia.html)
    <p> Contropedia provides a resource and tool to analyse and visualise ideological controversies on Wikipedia </p>

  * [The Fifa Files](projects/the-fifa-files.html)
    <p> The Sunday Times analysis of leaked files from the FIFA exposing one of the biggest scandals of soccer history </p>

  * [Lobby radar ](projects/lobby-radar.html)
    <p> 
Lobby Radar uncovers the lobbying groups and private interests in the backstage of German politics </p>

  * [a HÁLÓ](projects/a-hl.html)
    <p> a HÁLÓ exposes the connections between politicians, corporations and politicians and public funds attributed to them </p>

  * [Rise Project ](projects/rise-project.html)
    <p> The RISE Project reveals hidden connections between criminal organizations, politicians and businessmen in and around Romania </p>

  * [Justiciapedia](projects/justiciapedia.html)
    <p> Justiciapedia shows the connections between people working within the judicial system in Argentina </p>

  * [The Proxy Platform](projects/the-proxy-platform.html)
    <p> The Proxy Platform exposes proxy companies used for money laundering by crime lords and corrupt government beaurocrats in Eastern Europe </p>

  * [They Rule](projects/they-rule.html)
    <p> They Rule provides glimpses of some of the relationships of the US ruling class </p>

  * [China&#39;s African spending spree](projects/chinas-african-spending-spree.html)
    <p> This Al Jazeera investigation highlights African infrastructure financed by China </p>

  * [Open Corporates Financial Visualizations](projects/open-corporates-financial-visualizations.html)
    <p> Open Corporates Financial Visualisation shines a light the complex structures of 7 of the biggest corporations in the world </p>

  * [Follow The Money ](projects/follow-the-money.html)
    <p> Follow The Money is a free and verified archive of contributions to political campaigns in the US </p>

  * [Dollars for Docs](projects/dollars-for-docs.html)
    <p> Dollars for Docs shows details of financial payments from pharmaceutical companies to doctors in the US </p>

  * [NSA Surveillance Lawsuit Tracker](projects/nsa-surveillance-lawsuit-tracker.html)
    <p> ProPublica&#39;s NSA Surveillance Lawsuit tracker lists and gives that status of key lawsuits brought against the National Surveillance Agency for their surveillance activities  </p>

  * [Non Profit Explorer
](projects/non-profit-explorer.html)
    <p> Nonprofit explorer is a database showing the tax returns and financial details of tax exempt organisations </p>

  * [Boletín Oficial.info](projects/boletn-oficialinfo.html)
    <p> Boletín Oficial is a database and search tool for exploring contracts related to state budgets and public spending   </p>

  * [InfoAmazonia](projects/infoamazonia.html)
    <p> InfoAmazonia provides stories, news and data about the endangered Amazon region  </p>

  * [Siyazana](projects/siyazana.html)
    <p> Siyazana is a tool that allows users to investigate connections between the public and private spheres in South African politics </p>

  * [No Ceilings
](projects/no-ceilings.html)
    <p> No Ceilings compiles and visualises data on indicators of the participation of girls and women in everyday life  </p>

  * [OpenCorporates](projects/opencorporates.html)
    <p> OpenCorporates is the largest open database of companies in the world  </p>

  * [OpenInterests](projects/openinterests.html)
    <p> OpenInterests is a catalogue of political and commercial actors related to the European Union </p>

  * [Corpwatch Croctail](projects/corpwatch-croctail.html)
    <p> CrocTail provides an interface for browsing information about several hundred thousand U.S. publicly traded corporations and their foreign subsidiaries </p>

  * [Every Politician](projects/every-politician.html)
    <p> Every Politician aims to provide data about every politician in the world  </p>

  * [openAFRICA](projects/openafrica.html)
    <p> openAFRICA is an independent source for open data on the African continent </p>

  * [Secrecy for Sale: Inside the Global Offshore Money Maze](projects/secrecy-for-sale-inside-the-global-offshore-money-maze.html)
    <p> Secrecy for Sale is a multi-story exploration of data around the global offshore money maze  </p>

  * [Open Oil Corporate Networks](projects/open-oil-corporate-networks.html)
    <p> Open Oil Corporate Networks is an open data system to give an insight into the corporate structures that make up the oil, gas and mining industries  </p>

  * [InfluenceMap](projects/influencemap.html)
    <p> Influence Map measures and lists corporate influence on climate policy worldwide </p>

  * [K-Monitor](projects/k-monitor.html)

  * [LittleSis](projects/littlesis.html)

 </p>
