How can researchers, journalists and activists map out the influence of personal ties, economic interest and other relationships in politics?

The influence mappers community brings together power cartographers from across the world to

  • find out what works and what doesn't in data-driven social network analysis projects
  • develop a data standard that can be used to share data across systems
  • share technologies that help structure, visualize and analyze influence networks.

We will also bring together all of our expertise to map out the network of policy-makers involved in international trade negotiations around the regulation of intellectual property. This exploration will also serve as a first customer of the technologies developed in this project.
